\frame{
  \frametitle{Conclusions and Plan}
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Current systematic (10\%) covers change in total acceptance for T1bbbb, T2bb
  \item T2bb limit changes by less than 10\% (for fixed total acceptance)
  \item T1bbbb limit changes by more than 10\% at upper diagonal \\ for CTEQ6L1 to CTEQ6.6 (for fixed total acceptance)
  \item T1bbbb limit robust w.r.t change between MSTW 2008 and NNPDF2.1 (for fixed total acceptance)
  \item[]
  \end{itemize}
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Look at CT10 (ntuples half completed)
  \item Look at variations within a pdf set. 
  \end{itemize}
